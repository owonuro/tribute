LATEEF KAYODE JAKANDE

The man who transformed Lagos.

Life and Times of Jakande

  • 1929 - Born in the Epetedo area of Lagos Island.
  • 1934 - He started primary education at the Lagos public school at Enu-Owa, Lagos Island.
  • 1943 - He started his secondary education at King's College, Lagos but completed it at Ilesha Grammar School.
  • 1949 - He began a career in journalism first with the Daily Service and later joined the Nigerian Tribune which was owned by Chief Obafemi Awolowo.
  • 1975 - He established John West Publications and began to publish The Lagos News. He served as the first President of the Newspaper Proprietors Association of Nigeria (NPAN).
